// About Orgonite

What is Orgonite?

Orgonite is a powerful combination of organic and inorganic materials that work together to balance and harmonize bio-energy — also known as orgone, chi, or prana in various traditions.

The concept was developed by Wilhelm Reich, who identified orgone as a universal life force energy that permeates all living things. Orgonite devices are designed to attract, accumulate, and transmute this energy from negative (stagnant) to positive (flowing).

Each piece I create is handmade with intention — I choose crystals and metals based on their specific metaphysical properties, incorporate sacred geometry, and set intentions during the creation process that are literally embedded into the resin matrix.

Whether you're looking for EMF protection, abundance attraction, emotional healing, or simply a beautiful piece of art with purpose — there is an orgonite piece for you.
